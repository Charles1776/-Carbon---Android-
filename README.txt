Also see below for a quick setup guide
The Automated Tests were carried out using ZapTest mobile automation tool. Below are the steps for ZapTest Setup
1. Download and install Zaptest on your PC
2. Run Zaptest on your computer.
3. Download and unzip the Carbon Home challenge folder on this repository.
4. Click on the open project icon on Zaptest menu and select the unzipped Carbon Home Challenge project.
5. Download the Carbon Staging apk on your mobile device
6. Connect the mobile device(Android) to your PC via USB cable (Developer options should be enabled on mobile device)
7. Click on the Environment Manager (Cloud icon) on the Zaptest menu to connect to your mobile device
8. Click on Play/Run icon on the ZapTest menu.
